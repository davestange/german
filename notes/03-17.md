# Adjectives
Comparison - 
- adjective: add er
- Changes: add an umlaut to most one-syllable adjectives
	- o -> ö, a -> ä, u -> ü
- Changes: drop the final e in adjectives ending with -el or -er
	- teuer -> teurer
	- dunkel -> dunkler
- To form superlative of adjectives and adverbs, use the following formula
	- am + [adjective | adverb]+sten
	- if adjective ends with d, t, or s sound, add an e before the Sten ending
	- example: groß -> am größten
	- example: bunt -> am buntesten
	- example: intelligent -> intelligentesten
