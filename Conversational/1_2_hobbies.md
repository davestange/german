## 1.2 Hobbies (Hobbies / Freizeit)

### Key Phrases

| German | English |
|--------|---------|
| Was sind deine Hobbies? | What are your hobbies? |
| Meine Hobbies sind… | My hobbies are… |
| Was machst du in deiner Freizeit? | What do you do in your free time? |
| Und was machst du gerne? | And what do you enjoy doing? |

### Common Hobby Vocabulary

| German | English |
|--------|---------|
| Deutsch lernen | learning German |
| Podcasts hören | listening to podcasts |
| fotografieren | taking photos |
| joggen | jogging |
| Geige spielen | playing violin |
| Saxophon spielen | playing saxophone |
| lesen | reading |
| zeichnen | drawing |
| malen | painting |
| Puzzle spielen / puzzeln | doing puzzles |
| Schach spielen | playing chess |
| backen | baking |
| kochen | cooking |
| reisen | traveling |
| spazieren gehen | going for walks |
| Schmuck machen | making jewelry |
| Fußball gucken | watching football |

### Example Sentences

- *Meine Hobbies sind Deutsch lernen, Podcasts hören und fotografieren.*
- *Ich jogge gerne und spiele Geige.*
- *In meiner Freizeit backe ich und lese.*

### Grammar Tips

> **Verb Infinitives as Nouns:** In German, hobby activities are often listed as infinitives used like nouns (lowercase when in a list after "sind"):
> - *Meine Hobbies sind **lesen**, **kochen** und **reisen**.*

> **"spielen" = to play:** Used for both instruments and games:
> - *Geige **spielen*** (play violin) — instrument
> - *Schach **spielen*** (play chess) — game
